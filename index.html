About Palpatine

Sheev Palpatine, born and raised on the planet of Naboo, is the main antagonist in the Star Wars universe. He started his career as a politcian while training under his master, Darth Plagueis. He eventually worked himsel up into the galactic senate as a representative of Naboo, and later challenged the position of supreme chancellor Velorum.
